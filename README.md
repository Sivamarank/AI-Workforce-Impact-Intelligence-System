# AI Workforce Impact Intelligence System

## Project Overview

This project analyzes how Artificial Intelligence is transforming the workforce across industries through automation, salary impact, productivity changes, and workforce survival analysis.

The dashboard provides strategic insights into:

* AI replacement risk
* Workforce survival scores
* Salary impact after AI adoption
* Productivity and job satisfaction trends
* Upskilling requirements across industries

---

## Problem Statement

As AI adoption increases globally, industries are facing workforce transformation challenges. This project aims to identify:

* Which careers are most vulnerable to AI automation
* Which industries are adapting successfully
* How AI affects salaries and productivity
* The importance of workforce upskilling

## Key Features

### AI Survival Score

Custom workforce resilience scoring system based on:

* Automation risk
* Salary impact
* Productivity changes
* Job replacement status

### Workforce Risk Analytics

* High-risk industries
* Safest careers
* AI adoption analysis

### Productivity & Human Impact

* Productivity vs job satisfaction
* Remote work analysis
* Workforce adaptation trends

---

## Dashboard Insights

* Marketing industry showed the highest AI replacement exposure.
* Technical roles demonstrated stronger workforce resilience.
* AI adoption improved productivity in several industries.
* Upskilling emerged as a critical factor for workforce survival.
